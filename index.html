Projects

Dissertation Project

This project was designed, developed and tested for my dissertation project in university.

The hypothesis for this project was to examine the effectiveness of gamification in educational materials for undergraduate computing students with earlier research indicating that learning computer programming is difficult and may lead to high dropout rate (Dirzyte et al, 2023).

Software such as Figma, Miro, Visual Studio and SQL were used to design the fundamental layout and features and to store data from users and track their achievements.
